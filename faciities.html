Hospital Facilities

State-of-the-art medical facilities and equipment for comprehensive healthcare

Our Advanced Medical Facilities

Vidhya Hospital stands as Theni district's premier NABH accredited healthcare destination, offering comprehensive medical services through state-of-the-art facilities. Our multi-specialty approach ensures complete medical care under one roof, from routine check-ups to complex surgical procedures.

Located on Cumbum Main Road in Palanichettipatti, we serve the broader Theni region with specialized departments and accept major insurance schemes including CMCHIS and government employee health programs.

Laparoscopic Surgical Centre

Minimally Invasive Surgery Excellence

Our Laparoscopic Surgical Centre features high-definition systems and advanced instruments, enabling complex procedures through small keyhole incisions. This minimally invasive approach offers superior visualization while significantly reducing patient trauma and accelerating recovery times.

Advanced Equipment: 4K ultra-high-definition cameras, energy-based surgical devices, and specialized instruments with strict sterilization protocols following international safety standards.

Available Laparoscopic Procedures:
  • Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy (Gallbladder Removal)
  • Laparoscopic Appendectomy
  • LAVH (Laparoscopically Assisted Vaginal Hysterectomy)
  • Laparoscopic Hernia Repair
  • Laparoscopic Sterilization
  • Ureteric Stone Removal
Benefits of Laparoscopic Surgery:
  • Smaller incisions and minimal scarring
  • Faster recovery time
  • Reduced post-operative pain
  • Lower risk of infection
  • Shorter hospital stay

Dialysis Unit

Safe, Comfortable Renal Care
