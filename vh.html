ABOUT US

Vidhya Hospital

Since 1990, Vidhya Hospital has been serving Theni with excellence and compassion, providing world-class healthcare services right at your doorstep. Located on Cumbum Main Road in Palanichettipatti, our NABH-accredited hospital offers comprehensive medical care across 12+ specialties including cardiology, orthopedics, general surgery, and emergency services. We believe quality healthcare should be affordable and accessible to everyone, which is why our experienced team of doctors and modern facilities work together to deliver personalized treatment at transparent, budget-friendly prices. From routine checkups to complex surgeries, our 24/7 emergency services and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment ensure you and your family receive the best possible care when you need it most.
